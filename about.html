What courses do digital marketing institutes commonly offer for beginners?

Digital marketing institutes commonly offer a comprehensive beginner-friendly course that covers the fundamentals of online marketing. These courses usually start with an introduction to digital marketing concepts, followed by core modules such as search engine optimization (SEO), search engine marketing (Google Ads), social media marketing, and content marketing. Beginners are also taught email marketing, basic website and WordPress management, and web analytics using tools like Google Analytics to track and measure performance. Many institutes include additional topics such as affiliate marketing, e-commerce marketing, mobile marketing, and basic graphic design using tools like Canva. To enhance practical learning, these courses often include live projects, case studies, and industry-recognized certifications from platforms like Google, Meta, and HubSpot, making them suitable for students, job seekers, and entrepreneurs starting their digital marketing journey.
